LEONTEQ BY EFG // BRAND DESIGN

About

Leonteq is a Swiss company, active in the finance and technology sector. Based on modern technology, it offers products and services related to investments, covers capital protection, yield enhancement, and the participation of product classes.

Overview

Leonteq (formerly EFG Financial Products) which was launched in the Swiss Stock Exchange fall 2014, wanted to position itself as an independent provider of investment products.

Responsibilities

- Brand Design

Result

With Brandpulse I developed the new brand design for Leonteq, which defines a solid impression in the world of finance and investments.
