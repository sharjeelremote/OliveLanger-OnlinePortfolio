TEDXZURICH 2016 - PEOPLE FIRST // ART DIRECTION

About TEDx

TEDx brings people together to enjoy a unique experience, in the spirit of ideas worth spreading. At TEDxZurich, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussions and connections.

Overview

2016 was the second consecutive year as the Art Director of TEDxZurich. The motto of „People first“ conceptualized the intersection of technology, policy, design, and humanity.

Responsibilities

- Multi-Channel Design Concepts and Implementation
- Ensure the TEDx brand consistency and quality
- Connecting People through Creative Ideas
... and more

Result

In 2016, I continued my journey to create an unforgettable event experience for a multitude of people. Under consideration of the TEDx guidelines, I developed the look and feel of the conference. The aim was to illustrate the diversity as well as the uniqueness and beauty of humanity with the naked eye.
