Bank of Cyprus // Editorial Design

About

Bank of Cyprus is at an advanced stage of its transformation, becoming a stronger, outcome-driven organization. All updates and important changes are communicated in the SCR report.

Overview

Every year the Bank of Cyprus releases the CSR report (report for the institutions’ social corporate responsibility and self-regulation). The customer wanted an appealing way to visualize data and to show complex information in an easy way.

Responsibilities

- Editorial Design
- Data Visualization

Result

Based on the BOC brand guidelines I created the editorial design of the CSR report. A booklet, showing all the highlights and numbers in a simple and beautiful way, to keep the staff up to date.
