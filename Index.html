My name is Oliver Langner, and my passion is creating and managing professional and innovative solutions for progressive clients in various industries.

In recent years I have proven my expertise in marketing, branding, and communication design. Today I am expanding my knowledge in Design Systems, analytics, management, and the training of the next generation of creative talents at UBS.

WORK SAMPLES

In April 2019, I joined Lufthansa Systems to optimize the pilot navigation product experience for multiple airlines and develop the Design System for web and native applications. As the Design System Lead in the UI/UX team, I played a key role in global collaboration, guiding creative teams, managing digital assets, and ensuring visual consistency across platforms.
