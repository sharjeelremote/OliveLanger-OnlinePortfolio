NOVIS VITA JUICER // BRAND DESIGN

About

The worldwide operating company Novissa, a distributor of kitchen appliances, developed the “Vita Juicer”, a new kind of multifunctional juicer.

Overview

The client required a CI and CD for this kitchen gadget, to represent its uniqueness and transport the message of a natural and healthy lifestyle.

Responsibilities

- Brand Design
- Print and non Print Media
- Packaging Design
- POS Displays and Show Booths

Result

A refined logo that incorporates the main brand, the product, and its added value. The dynamic and vivid key visuals are stimulating the senses and highlight the USPs of the product. All integrated into the design of the packaging, show booths, and the digital and print media.
