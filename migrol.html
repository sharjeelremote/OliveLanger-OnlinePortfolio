Migrol // 2D and 3D guideline

About

Migrol is a Swiss oil company and a subsidiary of the Migros Group. Migrol's business area includes the operation of petrol stations, petrol station shops, heating oil sales, and related services.

Overview

The new design of Migrol gas stations made by Brandpulse had to be summarized in a clear guideline.

Responsibilities

- 3D and 2D Design Guideline

Result

In cooperation with Brandpulse I created the detailed guideline for Migrol, giving future design and architecture agencies the chance to easily adapt and understand the Migrol brand.
