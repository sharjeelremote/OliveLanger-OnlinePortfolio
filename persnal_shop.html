Personal shop // 12 Brands in 4 weeks

About

Personal Shop is granting selected members and employees the possibility of special purchasing conditions of a big variety of goods.

Overview

The online shop provides various brands, from well-known labels as well as their own. My task was to restructure and expand their brand portfolio.

Responsibilities

- Creation of 12 Brands in 4 Weeks

Result

With Brandpulse I created 12 brands in 4 weeks – six new fashion labels and the redesign of six existing ones. The results are 12 unique brand designs, including guidelines, packaging, and labeling.
